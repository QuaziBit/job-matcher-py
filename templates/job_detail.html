How are scores calculated?
Raw Score
LLM evaluates overall resume-to-job fit on a 1–5 scale.
Adjusted Score
Raw score minus penalties based on detected skill gaps.
Gap Type Penalty Cap Examples
Blocker −2 each max −3 Clearance, citizenship, mandatory certs
Major −1 each max −2 Significant missing skills
Minor −1 per 2 max −1 Nice-to-have or learnable gaps
Count −1 flat max −1 More than 6 total gaps
Example: Raw 4 · 1 blocker (−2) · 3 minors (−1) = Adjusted 1
